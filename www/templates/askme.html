Question: How can I improve my credit score?

System Answered: Pay your bills on time. Delinquent payments and collections can have a major negative impact on a credit score. Keep balances low on credit cards and other revolving credit. Apply for and open new credit accounts only as needed. Pay off debt rather than moving it around.
